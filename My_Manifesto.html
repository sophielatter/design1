What Is Creativity?




cre·a·tiv·i·ty
/ˌkrēāˈtivədē/
noun
the use of the imagination or original ideas, especially
in the production of an artistic work.


Creativity for me means just going for it. Making whatever comes
to my mind real by translating it to paper or in this case to the screen.
My process isn't really that complex.
1. Look for inspiration
2. Gather pictures or sketches
3. Jump into whatever program I need to use
4. Procrastinate
5. Scramble to get it done
6. Finish feeling accomplished
